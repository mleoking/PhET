How to Measure Distances Using Parallax

  1. With the mouse, move the reticle up or down until it overlays the star whose distance you want to measure.
  2. Using the joystick, line up the star with one of the vertical reticle lines.
  3. Press the Mark button to set the offset readout to 0.
  4. Move the slider left or right until the star you are measuring lines up with another vertical reticle line.
  5. Count the number of verticle reticle lines through which the star moved and enter this number in the Reticle Offset field.
  6. Click on the Compute button.
  7. The distance to the star will be displayed in the Distance field.
