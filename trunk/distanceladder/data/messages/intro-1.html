Lost In Space

Imagine that, while piloting your personal starship through the galaxy, your incompetent android assistant accidentaly pressed the Hyperspace Jump button and you suddenly found yourself lost in space.

Could you find your way back to your home solar system in time for the next episode of The Simpsons?

This game will give you the opportunity to navigate vast interstellar distances using the same techniques astronomers and cosmologists use to measure the size and age of the universe.

Strap on your space suit and climb aboard!
