Lost In Space

While piloting your personal space craft through the galaxy, your incompetent android assistant erroneously pressed the “hyperspace jump” button. You now find yourself in an unknown location, and need to navigate your way back to your home solar system before missing the next episode of The Simpsons.

Your ship is capable, through use of its new infinite improbability drive, of instantaneously moving to any point in the universe, so long as you know the distance to that point.

Your ship has two instruments that will help you measure interstellar distances. One uses the principle of parallax, which works for stars that aren’t too far away. The other measures the brightness of stars that you can see. By using them to measure distances to various stars, you can navigate to them, and eventually find your way home.
