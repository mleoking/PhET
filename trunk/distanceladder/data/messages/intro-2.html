The Cockpit

This is the display you will see first. You can get to it at any time by clicking on the Cockpit tab near the top of the screen.

Maneuvering

You can change the direction the ship is pointed by moving the Turn slider near the top of the control panel to the right of the cockpit.

The joystick moves the ship left and right, only.

Your ship has a hyperdrive that can make it jump forward a specified distance. Its controls are located on the panel to the right of the cockpit

Measuring Distances

Your ship has two instruments that will help you measure interstellar distances. Buttons for activating these instruments are on the cockpit's dashboard. Their controls and readouts are on the right.

  • The Parallax Instrument uses the principle of parallax, which works for stars that aren't too far away.
  • The Photometer measures the brightness of stars that you can see. This is most useful for measuring the distances to certain types of stars that are too far away to measure using parallax.

By using these instruments to measure distances to various stars, you can navigate to them, and eventually find your way home.
