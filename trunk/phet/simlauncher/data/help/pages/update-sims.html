Updating Simulations

Check all installed simulations for updates

Go to the Add/Remove/Update Sims tab, and look at the list of simulations. The third column tells you if an update is available for any simulations that you have installed on your computer. If there is a red exclamation mark (!) in this column, there is an update available.

Update all simulations

Go to the Add/Remove/Update Sims tab, and click on the All simulations category on the left side. Select all the simulations that have updates available (indicated by the red exclamation mark in the third column) by clicking on the small box just to the left of the simulations. Then click the Install/Update button at the bottom of the screen.

Turn off automatic update check

In the Options pulldown menu at the top of the screen, you will see an option to Automatically check for updates on startup. If the automatic update is on, there will be a checkmark next to this option. To turn it off, just click on it and the checkmark will go away.
