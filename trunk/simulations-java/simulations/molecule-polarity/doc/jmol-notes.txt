Notes related to Jmol feasibility tests for molecule-polarity

Feature requirements:
+1 atoms
+1 bonds
+1 ball-and-stick
+1 bond dipoles
+1 molecular dipole
partial charges
background color (preferably transparent)
+1 electrostatic potential
rotate to align with E-field
custom surfaces (for KP's electron density view)
custom colors for atoms, bonds, dipoles, surfaces
customization of dipoles (arrow size, tail size, scale, position)
+1 hide watermark
+1 hide popup menu
