Getting Links to Sims

Educators often develop worksheets or presentations in which they would like to insert links that will launch simulations. This requires finding the location of the simulation and getting a URL for it.

PhET SimLauncher installs simulations in the following directory:

Windows

C:\PhET\sl-local-root\www.colorado.edu\physics\phet\simulations

Mac

/PhET/sl-local-root/www.colorado.edu/physics/phet/simulations

Most simulations have their own directory under this simulations directory. Some simulations are part of a family that has a subdirectory (e.g., Lasers, Discharge Lamps and Photoelectric Effect). These subdirectories hold the launch files for the simulations. The launch files for Java simulations end with .jnlp, and the launch files for Flash simulations end with .swf.
