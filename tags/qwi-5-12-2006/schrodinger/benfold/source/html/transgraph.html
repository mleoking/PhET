Transmission and Reflection

This applet graphs transmission and reflection against energy, for a series of potential barriers. The potential function is shown on the left-hand side of the applet; the height, width, period and number of barriers can be adjusted using the fields provided.

The right-hand graph shows reflection and transmission (either or both may be plotted) versus energy. The range of energy values plotted can also be chosen.

Notes

  • The potential graph will only display a few barriers; the purpose is simply to represent the shape of the function
  • Switching between plotting transmission/reflection/both does not require a significant delay; changing other parameters will result in a calculation delay proportional to the number of barriers
  • To cancel a calculation, click the graph
  • The controls will be disabled during longer calculations
