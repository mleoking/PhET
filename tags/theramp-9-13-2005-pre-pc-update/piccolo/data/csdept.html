Welcome to the Web server for the Department of Computer Science at
the University of Maryland, College Park.

Our department is consistently ranked in the top 20 CS departments; our 38 faculty members have received over 20 NSF PYI/NYI/Career awards, a Packard, a Sloan and a Presidential Faculty Fellowship, and many have been appointed ACM/IEEE/AAAS/AAAI Fellows. The CS department is located in the AV Williams building at UMCP. The University of Maryland, College Park, is located inside the DC Beltway, minutes away from NASA Goddard, and convenient to downtown DC, northern Virginia, and Baltimore.

On Saturday, June 12, Dr. John Gannon, CS Chairman and beloved professor, passed away.
